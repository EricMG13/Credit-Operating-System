## Persona red flags resolved

- **Buy-side analyst:** no Model, Deep-Dive, evidence, QA, ASK, layout, export or spreadsheet capability was removed. The working model now saves before checkpoint creation, so an unavailable context cannot strand analyst work.
- **PM/CIO:** Command preserves the portfolio/coverage modules but leads with one ranked primary decision. Reference/demo content remains visibly non-live.
- **Head of Research / QA:** conclusions expose authority; Monitor events are durable; source/model/report artifacts are versioned and analyst-owned; publication rejects reference or incomplete inputs.
- **Phone user:** Model and Deep-Dive no longer pretend to be authoring workstations at 390px. They provide read-only posture/authority summaries and desk handoffs.
