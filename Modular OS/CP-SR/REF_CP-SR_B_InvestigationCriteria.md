# REF_CP-SR_B | Investigation Criteria & Scoring Rubric
# Version: 2.0 | 2026-06-10 | Evidence chain requirement added (from CP-2 analytical standard)

## EVIDENCE CHAIN REQUIREMENT
Every material dimension finding and every dimension score must connect: **[Evidence]** (sourced sector datapoint, filing figure, rating action, market level, or documented trend) → **[Risk Mechanic]** (how it transmits into sector PD, margin durability, FCF, liquidity, refinancing capacity, or recovery) → **[Credit Implication]** (mapped to a canonical value per REF_CP-SR_F). Generic sector commentary without a credit mechanic must be removed. Missing evidence → [Insufficient Information], reflected in the Confidence assignment — never converted into an adverse or favorable score.

## INVESTIGATION DIMENSIONS

### Dimension 1: Market Structure, Size & Growth Dynamics
| Factor | Assessment Focus |
|---|---|
| Market size | Total addressable market, revenue pool, subscriber base |
| Growth trajectory | Organic growth, ARPU, volume, pricing |
| Market maturity | Saturation, penetration, technology adoption |
| Fragmentation | Number of players, HHI proxy, consolidation potential |
| Structural shifts | Technology migration, convergence, wholesale models |

### Dimension 2: Key Credit Risk Drivers
| Factor | Assessment Focus |
|---|---|
| Cyclicality | Macro sensitivity, revenue beta, consumer pressure |
| Leverage norms | Sector leverage range by sub-segment |
| Margin pressure | EBITDA margin trend, cost inflation, pricing power |
| Cash conversion | FCF/EBITDA, capex intensity, working capital |
| Earnings quality | Recurring vs one-off, addbacks, accounting adjustments |

### Dimension 3: Competitive Landscape
| Factor | Assessment Focus |
|---|---|
| Concentration | Top competitor share, national vs regional dynamics |
| Barriers to entry | Spectrum, licenses, infrastructure capex |
| Disruption risk | New entrants, substitutes, technology displacement |
| Pricing dynamics | Rationality, churn, promotional intensity |
| Competitive moats | Network ownership, scale, brand, switching costs |

### Dimension 4: Regulatory & Macro Factors
| Factor | Assessment Focus |
|---|---|
| Regulatory regime | Spectrum policy, wholesale access, merger approvals |
| Political risk | State intervention, national champions, legal risk |
| Macro sensitivity | Rates, inflation, FX, energy, tariffs |
| ESG / sustainability | Energy usage, digital inclusion, climate investment |
| Policy direction | EU Digital Decade, broadband targets, fair-share debates |

### Dimension 5: Capital Structure Trends & Funding Conditions
| Factor | Assessment Focus |
|---|---|
| Leverage trajectory | Deleveraging / releveraging path |
| Refinancing conditions | Maturity wall, spreads, market access |
| Capital allocation | Dividends, buybacks, M&A, debt reduction |
| Funding mix | Bonds, loans, private credit, secured/unsecured |
| Infrastructure monetization | Tower/fibre JVs, carve-outs, asset recycling |

### Dimension 6: Early Warning & Stress Indicators
| Factor | Assessment Focus |
|---|---|
| Default trends | TTM defaults, distressed exchanges, repeat defaults |
| Downgrade momentum | Outlook bias, rating migration, watchlist additions |
| Market stress signals | Spreads, CDS, loan prices, equity drawdowns |
| Liquidity indicators | RCF usage, covenant headroom, cash runway |
| Operational stress | Subscriber loss, ARPU decline, capex overruns |

## SCORING RUBRIC
| Score | Label | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Very Favorable | Strong structural tailwind; positive credit trajectory |
| 2 | Favorable | Modest positive dynamics; stable to improving |
| 3 | Neutral | Balanced risk/opportunity; no clear directional bias |
| 4 | Unfavorable | Emerging headwinds; deteriorating trajectory |
| 5 | Very Unfavorable | Material structural risk; negative credit trajectory |

## CONFIDENCE ASSIGNMENT
| Level | Criteria |
|---|---|
| High | At least 3 corroborating Tier 1/2 sources; no material conflicts |
| Medium | 1–2 Tier 1/2 sources or Tier 3 with partial corroboration |
| Low | Single source, Tier 3/4 only, or material data conflicts |
